How It Works Sheriff Cash

Here’s how it works:

Deposit & Obfuscation

Incoming funds are split into randomized amounts and sent through multiple entry addresses, preventing direct traceability.

Sequential Redistribution

Funds are transferred through intermediary wallets with randomized time delays, ensuring no predictable transaction patterns.

Exchange Liquidity Injection

A portion of the funds is routed through high-liquidity exchanges, eliminating on-chain history and breaking transaction links.

Clean Wallet Distribution

Mixed funds are sent from pre-prepared clean wallets to the final recipient in randomized amounts and intervals.
